Playground

Step into our expansive playground and let the fun begin! With a multitude of structures, a spacious field, and a sprawling blacktop area, there is no shortage of excitement and adventure for students during recess.

But that's not all - when winter coats the landscape, brace yourself for double the delight with not one, but two sledding hills. At our playground, every season brings an abundance of joy and endless opportunities for unforgettable memories!
