Art Room

Welcome to the vibrant and inspiring Art Room at Kromrey Middle School, a place where creativity knows no bounds. The moment you step into the room, you'll be greeted by a colorful and inviting space that sparks your imagination. Here, students have the opportunity to express themselves through a variety of art techniques, including painting, drawing, collage, and sculpture.

Our art room is equipped with the latest art supplies and tools, opening up endless possibilities for our students to explore their inner artist. From unleashing their creativity to learning new techniques, students can embrace their love for art and create stunning works to showcase their talent.
