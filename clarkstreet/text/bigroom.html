The Big Room

This big room is easily convertible to accommodate groups of all sizes for a variety of needs. Clark Street uses this space for ‘all school’ meetings, two separate classrooms, breaking groups into smaller groups, etc.

The room dividers have whiteboard surfaces that allow our learners to make their thinking visible and to express their creativity.

The breakout spaces throughout our school are useful for students who prefer quieter spaces, or need to work with a teacher while everyone else is working quietly. The flexible and easily movable furniture in all of our spaces help us quickly redesign the room to best accommodate the learning needs.
