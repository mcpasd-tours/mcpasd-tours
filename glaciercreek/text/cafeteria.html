Cafeteria

Welcome to the heart of Glacier Creek Middle School: the cafeteria. Step into this open and inviting space where students gather to refuel and connect with their peers. Take a moment to admire the murals adorning the walls, showcasing student artwork and celebrating our school's creativity. Don't miss the prominent ROAR poster displayed proudly, emphasizing the core values of our school community: Respectful, Optimistic, Academic, and Responsible.
