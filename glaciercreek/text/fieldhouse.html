Field House

The Fieldhouse at Glacier Creek Middle School is a spacious and versatile gymnasium that serves as a hub for physical education classes, basketball games, and other athletic activities. With its basketball hoops and marked court, students can engage in intense and competitive matches while developing their skills. The adaptable layout allows for a variety of activities, such as fitness classes and dance rehearsals, and the floor ensures a safe and comfortable environment. Spectators can cheer on their peers from the seating area, creating an energetic and supportive atmosphere that inspires students to stay active and foster a love for sports and physical fitness.
