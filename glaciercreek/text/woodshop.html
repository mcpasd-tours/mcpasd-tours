Woodshop

The Woodshop classroom at a Glacier Creek Middle School is a dynamic and hands-on learning environment where students learn the art of woodworking. This well-equipped room features sturdy workbenches, a variety of hand tools, and machinery such as a table saw and band saw. Safety is prioritized with clear guidelines, safety equipment, and an extraction system. Under the guidance of an experienced instructor, students develop woodworking skills while creating a range of projects. From basic birdhouses to intricate furniture, this space fosters creativity, craftsmanship, and problem-solving skills, providing students with a sense of pride and accomplishment as they transform raw materials into beautiful and functional objects.
