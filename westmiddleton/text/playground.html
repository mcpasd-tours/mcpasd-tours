Playground

Welcome to the newly constructed playground at West Middleton Elementary School!

This fantastic playground was made possible with funds from the 2022 'Wolverine Dash' as well as contributions from the school and district.

The project was designed with all students in mind. It includes various adaptive features, such as a wheelchair-friendly 'wolverine market' play structure, a rubber surface path leading to the playground, and even musical instruments for everyone to enjoy.
