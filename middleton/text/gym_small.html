Small Gym

Step into the Small Gym at Middleton High School, a versatile space that accommodates a range of activities. This area serves as the venue for gymnastics, ropes, and other PE classes, along with that it serves as a training space for the cheer and dance programs. This space offers students opportunities to stretch their limits and develop their physical abilities. Whether you're navigating the ropes course or engaging in invigorating PE sessions, the Small Gym provides a conducive environment for growth, sport, and exploration.
