North Library Media Center

Greetings and welcome to the stunning North Library Media Center at Middleton High School! This elegant space boasts private rooms that students can check out to study in privacy and comfort. The expansive area is surrounded by big windows that allow ample natural light and provide a charming view. The library houses a myriad of books, catering to a variety of interests and curiosity. Also, students have access to an advanced computer lab that empowers them to engage in research, write papers and more. Come explore the North Library Media Center, a space brimming with knowledge and possibilities.
