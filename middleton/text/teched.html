Welcome to the Engineering Department at Middleton High School! In this dynamic space, students have the opportunity to explore and experience various types of engineering through project-based learning. They can delve into Advanced Manufacturing and Automation, honing their skills in CNC machining and Robotics. Additionally, they can earn industry-recognized certifications in Computer-Aided Design and Robotics.

Our well-equipped facility provides access to industry-level machines, including Tormach CNC mills and lathes, a Fanuc robotic arm, and Form Labs 3D printers. This collaborative and innovative environment empowers students to unleash their potential and prepares them for future careers in engineering.
