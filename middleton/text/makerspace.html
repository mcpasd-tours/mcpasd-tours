Makerspace

The Maker Space at Middleton High School is a collaborative environment where students, teachers, and community members can come together to incubate ideas, innovate, problem-solve, and think critically. Equipped with a wide range of tools and materials such as sewing machines, robotics, and arts and crafts supplies, the space provides opportunities for students to explore textiles, electronics, art, and more.

It fosters a supportive and empowering environment where individuals can create and share their talents or simply explore new ideas. The space is currently home to a vibrant 'maker space club' where students eagerly play, make, learn, and facilitate lessons.
