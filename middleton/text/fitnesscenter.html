Fitness Center

Welcome to the Fitness Center at Middleton High School! Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, specifically designed to meet the fitness needs of our students and staff. Whether you are a student wanting to stay active during Physical Education (PE) classes or a staff member looking for an invigorating workout, our Fitness Center has something for everyone.

Designed with the latest fitness trends in mind, we offer a wide range of equipment to cater to various fitness goals. From cardio machines such as treadmills, stationary bikes, and ellipticals, to strength training equipment including weight machines and free weights, our Fitness Center has everything you need to stay fit and healthy.
