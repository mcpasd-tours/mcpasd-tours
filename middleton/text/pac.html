Performing Arts Center

The Middleton Performing Arts Center Auditorium is a 899 seat theater operated by the Middleton-Cross Plains Area School district. It was opened in December 2002 and upgraded in 2020 with new seating, lighting, and sound systems. The auditorium hosts events for Middleton High School and other local groups, and is available for rent by the community.

For information on how to reserve the PAC visit our website. https://www.mcpasd.k12.wi.us/page/performing-arts-center.
