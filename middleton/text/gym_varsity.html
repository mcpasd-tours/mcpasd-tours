Varsity/Competition Gym

Welcome to the Varsity/Competition Gym at Middleton High School! This versatile space is the go-to venue for all of our exciting games and competitions. As you step inside, prepare to be amazed by the beautiful Cardinal, proudly displayed in the center of the floor. The Varsity/Competition Gym hosts a variety of teams, including thrilling basketball, volleyball, and wrestling matches. It not only serves as the epicenter for our athletic events but also functions as the central hub for physical education classes. With comfortable bleachers and a prominent scoreboard, spectators can cheer on their favorite teams in style. Whether you're a passionate fan, a dedicated athlete, or a student participating in PE, the Varsity/Competition Gym is where impressive athletic performances come alive.
