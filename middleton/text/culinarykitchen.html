Culinary Kitchen

Welcome to the Culinary Kitchen!

Here, we provide professional-style kitchen spaces for students to learn valuable skills across various disciplines such as culinary arts, nutrition, and childcare. Whether you're aspiring to become a chef, nutritionist or childcare teacher, there is something for everyone in our department.

We are excited to introduce several new courses, including a baking and pastry class, where the baked goods created by students will be used for students to enjoy in the school cafeteria.

We have also collaborated with the social studies department to create a new course titled 'Culture and Cuisine.' Did you know that the FCS Department has helped 164 students obtain their ACCT [Assistant Childcare Teacher] certificates? This allows students the opportunity to begin a career in the childcare field.
