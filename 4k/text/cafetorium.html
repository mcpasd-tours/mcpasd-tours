Gym/Cafetorium

The Gym/Cafetorium at our Early Learning Center is a versatile and dynamic space that serves multiple purposes for our 4k program. This flexible area is designed to accommodate physical activities, dining, and various other events.

During meal times, the Gym/Cafetorium transforms into a comfortable and spacious dining area. Here, children enjoy nutritious meals and have the opportunity to socialize with their peers and teachers.

Additionally, the Gym/Cafetorium serves as a venue for special events, such as assemblies, performances, and parent meetings. It provides a large and welcoming space that can accommodate a larger audience and allows for flexibility in hosting various activities and programs.
