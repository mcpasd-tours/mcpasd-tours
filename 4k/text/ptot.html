Occupational Therapy (OT) and Physical Therapy (PT) Space

The OT/PT Space at the Early Learning Center is a dedicated area designed to support the occupational therapy (OT) and physical therapy (PT) needs of our students. It is an inviting and inclusive space that is tailored to promote growth, development, and overall well-being.

Equipped with specialized equipment and resources, the OT/PT Space provides a supportive environment for children to engage in therapeutic activities that address their unique needs. From fine motor skill development to gross motor coordination, the space is designed to facilitate progress in various areas of physical and cognitive development.
