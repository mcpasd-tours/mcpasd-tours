Health Office

The Health Office is an important component of our 4k program as it provides a safe and healthy environment for the children. Our Health Office is staffed by a certified nurse who is dedicated to ensuring the health and well-being of all children in our program.

The Health Office is equipped to handle a wide variety of medical needs, including administering medication, responding to medical emergencies, and treating minor injuries. It's also a resource for parents and caregivers who have questions or concerns about their child's health.
