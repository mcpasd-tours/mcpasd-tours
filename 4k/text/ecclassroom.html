Early Childhood (EC) Classroom

The Early Childhood (EC) classroom at the Early Learning Center is a specialized and inclusive learning environment designed to meet the unique needs of students aged 3-4 years who have been identified with a disability.

This classroom is thoughtfully structured to create a nurturing and supportive atmosphere where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. With a focus on individualized instruction and a multidisciplinary approach, our dedicated staff collaborates to provide high-quality education and support for each student.
