Front Office/Vestibule

Welcome to our building! As you step inside, you are in our vestibule area, which visitors enter once buzzed in through the front entrance.

These doors provide access to either the front office or the school, and we have implemented several security measures to prioritize the safety and well-being of all staff, students, and visitors.

Our visitor management system is one of these measures, requiring everyone to sign in and receive a visitor badge. This system helps us maintain a record of who is in the building at any given time, ensuring that only authorized individuals can access specific areas.
